# Chapter 7 Outline: Whispernight (Galeran)

**Status:** DRAFTED (pending author review — see
`../../draft/chapter-07-draft-notes.md`) | **Date:** 14th of Ashenfall
(Day of Hollow / Whispernight), 982 A.M. | **Place:** Dovelea Market,
eastern Halreth

Act One's parallel Galeran chapter (handoff §6): establish his status in
Halreth while the Glintgate arc runs.

## Single evening, one location
- Status established: dockhand under the name "Garret," three carp a day,
  papered out of licensed work by a Merchant's Guild writ that wants him
  *placeable*, not jailed
- Whispernight: the lantern town, confessions given to the millstream; both
  moons rendered (standing rule) — the broken one brighter, tripled shadows
- Naeryn strings three new mourning charms (Rusk, Thatch, Thom); the
  grief/guilt engine stated: "Lyra Ounvian had given the orders. He had
  built the door she walked through."
- Galeran refuses the confession ritual; the burned insignia named as the
  account he keeps ("You keep everything in the arm.")
- The hunt arrives: four false rivermen at the amnesty stone asking about
  grey hair + a fifth watcher on the Serenehold road — Anverus, at distance,
  never named in-scene
- Naeryn's disclosures (seeding Act Two, not spending it): the masking sigil
  — "The rune taught me"; on the night all chroma runs slow, the
  rift-touched place in her doesn't; the pull east "does not call. It
  assumes."
- Decision: out before the lanterns come down, north past the weir, then
  east on their own terms; Galeran demands the full account once clear —
  "I want to walk the ground before it closes."
- Close: "the squad leader" returns; two shadows and then six under the
  broken moon

## End state
- Galeran: moving again, commanding again, carrying knowledge nobody else
  has (Lyra, the rift, the Naaren encampment) and a company he can't stop
  belonging to
- Naeryn: masking them at a cost; the eastward pull named but unexplained
- Anverus: one step behind, west road watched — the Act Two hunt is live
