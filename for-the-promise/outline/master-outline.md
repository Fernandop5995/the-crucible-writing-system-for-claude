## The Council's True Play (canon locked)

Surface: scholars sent to investigate arcane phenomena in the Vel'Era forests
have gone silent; Vel'Era's borders are closed; the recovery must be discreet.
Truth: the Wyrshade's mind-reading gave the Throng Elyrieth's location; the
council secretly sent scholars toward the drake; they vanished. Caltheran
cannot send soldiers (act of war) and cannot confess (the fiasco). The
Promise is the deniable asset — and the pre-built scapegoat: they delivered
the Wyrshade (on record), so if they're caught in Vel'Era the narrative
writes itself. Damien's public hostility pre-builds it. Tauvor implies the
Wyrshade link subtly, says the forest "may contain the key to the rift," and
does NOT name Elyrieth.

## State of the World Entering Book 2 (canon)

- The siege of Caer Seryth lasted a week; the south wall fell; **Mark was
  lost to the Order**; Tella scarred (artifice leg by Lune — revealed in
  later chapters)
- Judas, Celisia, and Mark went east and **took the Vigil**: the Kymancy
  Rift is under joint Order/Naaren control; the Halreth delegates there are
  dead — Halreth is on the verge of declaring war
- Vel'Era closed its borders between books; Elaine refused Aunt Lysandria's
  political marriage offer; she lost her left pinky in the siege
- Phoebe commands Ferrena's old squad (the Hollow, Julissa, Carmen, Mirian,
  Lorian); Ferrena promoted to captain with a new squad
- The House Pevlium invitation falls in **winter 982** (timeskip to it)

## Handoff Conflicts (author must rule)

1. **Chapter 5's council scene.** Drafted (from the roadmap): Tauvor's full
   confession — vivisection horror, the butchery, the voice — and an *escort
   contract for a new expedition*. Handoff (§5): subtle implication, no
   Elyrieth, no full confession, and the ask is *discreet recovery of
   scholars already missing* behind closed borders. Also §2.4 gives the
   vivisection a different mechanism (mind-read → brain death) than the
   roadmap's madness → butchery → the burning voice.
2. **Chapter 6 exists outside the five-POV lock.** The roadmap made
   Frederick the ch-6 lens; the handoff's canon lock lists five POVs without
   him. The wife-meeting and hunt content also has no anchor in the handoff's
   act plan (Damien's landing point: UNDECIDED).
3. **Venue wording**: handoff §5.1 puts the council meeting "at the Golden
   Scales guildhall"; the written ch-5 prose puts it at the College of
   Cvostral (and the guildhall belongs to Damien's thread). Prose likely
   governs, but confirm.
