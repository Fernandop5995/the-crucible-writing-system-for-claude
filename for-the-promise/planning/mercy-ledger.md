## The Mercy Engine

Acts of compassion that seem costly in the moment and pay off at the Apex.
The Crucible Structure expects roughly four, seeded across Movements II-IV,
all converging at the climax.

## Possible Seeds Already On Page **[PROPOSED — author to confirm or discard]**

1. **The messenger at Bordou Square (Ch 2).** Brunor put a dagger to his
   throat and did not cut. If the messenger is a person and not a prop, he has
   seen the Promise choose restraint inside a hostile city. Cheap to activate
   later.
2. **Damien's blindness (Open Threads).** "He doesn't know what's standing in
   his city." Warning him — giving an enemy the truth for nothing — would be a
   textbook costly mercy, and it would force the answer to whether his
   ignorance protects or dooms him.
3. **Beatrice (Ch 5a).** Small kindnesses to the watching serving girl are the
   classic low-status mercy whose payoff is information.
