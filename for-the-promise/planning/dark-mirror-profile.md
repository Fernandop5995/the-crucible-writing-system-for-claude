## Book 2 Usage **[PROPOSED — author to confirm]**

- The Glintgate arc's active mirror is **Caelus vs Elaine**: Celisia's
  deliberate reveal is the knock on the door; the roadmap already plants that
  the void at the gate may have been his influence. If Book 2's fire strand is
  the widening gap between what Elaine knows and what she can say, Caelus —
  who weaponizes silence itself — is the thematic payoff.
- **Judas vs Brunor** reads as the series-level mirror (the sheets give Judas
  the Shepherd/disappointed-father iconography opposite Johanna's shepherding
  legacy). Book 2 can seed him without spending him.
- The Order's **anonymous true leader** sits above the web — reserve.

## The Antagonist's Truth **[TBD]**

No source yet states the Order's coherent philosophy — what makes the Wyrm
right from inside. The helmet lore (thirteen drake-helms forged, three
unfilled, a fourteenth that "no mortal could bear") implies the Order believes
it is *completing* something. The Crucible Structure needs this argument
stated somewhere the protagonist can be tempted by it.
