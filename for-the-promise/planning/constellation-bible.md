## Johanna's Promise (the company)

Mercenary/contract company named for Johanna. Takes protection work ("exactly
the kind of job the Promise takes," Ch 5b). Delivered the Wyrshade to the
Council (Open Threads). Field trio in Glintgate: Brunor, Elaine, Frederick.
Home keep: Caer Seryth (Ch 3).
