## Cosmology in one breath

Planet: **Eōtria** — a living goddess. Her husband-protector: **Solsyrr**, the
sun god. Above all: **Ubil**, planet-hunter and god-eater, true source of
Chroma. **The Merge** (Year 0): Ubil's seed struck the world, flooding it with
raw magic — "A.M." = After the Merge. Chroma fractured natural law into the
**thirteen pillars**; a mythical fourteenth (the Spiral, Creation) belongs to
Eōtria's own line. **Primordial magic** (silver, pre-spectrum — Vel'Era's
forests) predates and opposes Chroma.

**The Throng**: Ubil's hive — infects, assimilates, repurposes biomass; cannot
take a living person warded by Abjuration, but the dead turn. **The fourteen
Rune-Drakes**: Eōtria's sleeping guardians, reservoirs of pure magic; the
Throng corrupts a found drake toward a forced awakening as a Throng Commander
that opens its Rift. **The Runes**: Eōtria's countermeasure, placed inside
threatened Rifts. **The Riftmarked**: descendants of the twelve Legendary
Heroes, chosen by Runes; only they can claim a Rune and kill the corresponding
corrupted drake. Awakened Riftmarked are immortal — except the Spiral.

**Book 2's engine (canon)**: the Wyrshade vivisection (981 A.M.) started a
**five-year countdown to the awakening of Elyrieth, the Kymancy Drake**. The
Kymancy Rift sits at the **Shadowfen** (Naaren marshes, southwest, near the
Caltheran border — east of Caer Seryth). Nobody in-world knows the clock is
running. The Order of the Wyrm manipulates events to facilitate the awakening.
Elaine is the Riftmarked of Kymancy; the Hollow is the Riftmarked of the
Spiral.

**[FLAG — dates]** The compendium places Book 2 in **983-985 A.M.**; the
manuscript datelines read **8th of Ashenfall, 982 A.M.** (and Book 1 is
981-982). Reconcile before drafting new datelines.

## Standing rules that touch Book 2 drafting

- **Two moons: Luuna and Sol.** One intact; one visibly shattered, its pieces
  drifting, and the broken one shines *brighter*, with strange scattered
  shadows. Every night scene must account for both (author's standing rule).
  **[FLAG]** Ch 1's campfire scene mentions "the two moons" without the broken
  moon — revision opportunity.
- Iskari doctrine: the broken moon is a dead god's body, still radiating.
- The Ebonmaw Ocean (north) — no ship returns from deep water; Marisport is
  Caltheran's only, grim, port.
- Silver hair marks Eōtria's bloodline (the Hollow, Wilhiem Neorxn, Miraen
  Vos — open thread).
- Weather is chroma-touched and mechanically real (see compendium tables);
  Chroma typhoons form in the Veiled Expanse and hit Vel'Era first — the
  Book 1 typhoon that nearly destroyed Caer Seryth formed there.

## The Wyrshade Disaster (Council confession)

The Promise delivered the Wyrshade (Merea led the escort — sheets). Under
vivisection (981 A.M.) it cast a Kymancy vision assault — the Rift, the rune,
what waits inside — that broke fifteen researchers: the butchery, the burning,
and one voice speaking an unknown tongue through a dying man ("something
answered"). First Era records point to something old sleeping in Vel'Era's
deep forests; the Council's expedition contract conceals a slumbering
**drake**. **[TBD]** Which drake sleeps in Vel'Era — the Kymancy drake's rift
is at the Shadowfen, so the Vel'Era sleeper is presumably a *different* drake
(fourteen exist; three helmets are missing). Author to confirm what the
Council knows vs what is true.

  Throng. The five-year countdown now has a destination.

  Promise's mission is a border violation by design (scapegoat architecture).

  Naeryn's arc begins revealing it.
