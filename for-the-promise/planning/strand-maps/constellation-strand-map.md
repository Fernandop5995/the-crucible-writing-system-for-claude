## The Constellation

Johanna's Promise — the company as the bond network. Field trio: Brunor,
Elaine, Frederick. Home front at Caer Seryth: Phoebe (student), Tella (mentor).
The name itself is the constellation's origin: Johanna, the watchtower in
Hudria, her words about the stone, about legacy, about what it means to carry
a name.

---

## Bond State by Chapter (attested)

### Ch 1 — Brunor & Elaine on the road
Campfire in the Blackwood Forest; the clearing, the rabbits, the two moons.
Brunor's flashback to Johanna. Frederick already waiting inside Glintgate.

### Ch 2 — Protection under pressure
Brunor puts a dagger to the messenger's throat when Elaine is threatened;
accepts a contest of arms when Damien draws on her.

### Ch 3 — The home front (Phoebe POV)
Phoebe and Tella: mentor and student, rough warmth and hard truths. Phoebe
worries about Elaine walking into Damien's city with Brunor's restlessness and
Frederick barely cleared by West. Tella reminds her who trained Elaine.

### Ch 4 — The fracture
After the duel: the rain scene. Elaine's fury, Brunor's stubbornness, the
argument about Alessa and what he kept from her. Ends in silence.

### Ch 5a — The debt
Frederick admits he knew about Alessa; refuses to let Brunor take the blame
alone. "Two ropes. I'd rather neither, but I'm a quartermaster. I count what's
there." Beatrice the serving girl eyeing Frederick.

### Ch 5b — Three readings of one horror
Brunor goes still the way he does when something changes the shape of a war.
Frederick does the arithmetic behind his eyes. Elaine carries knowledge she
can't voice. The trio leaves with more questions than they walked in with.

### Ch 6 — Trust exercised as separation
Brunor goes into the city alone — told Elaine, barely; didn't tell Frederick.
Elaine let him go because she needed that thread pulled and couldn't do it
herself. Frederick holds the guildhall meeting together: the wall behind
Brunor's spine and the ground beneath Elaine's feet, both at once. Frederick
gets agency outside the Brunor-Elaine conflict for the first time.
