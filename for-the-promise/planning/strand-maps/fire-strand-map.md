## The Fire

**Bearer:** Elaine.

**Form:** The riftmark. Attested behavior in chapters 1-6:
- Floods her with a room's emotional noise (the tavern: farmhands, the
  merchant counting coins, the woman watching the stairs)
- Opens on its own the moment she crosses a threshold (the Council chamber)
- Reads fractures beneath performance: fear, resentment, buried guilt across
  the Thirteen; which Wardens already knew the full account and which didn't
- Distinguishes performed emotion from real (Tauvor's genuine fear beneath
  composure; "not a man performing fear")
- Puts weight behind every word she speaks; Damien is afraid of her
- Detects absence as well as presence: the void where a person should be
  (Celisia at the gate — or Caelus's influence; unresolved)

**Related knowledge:** Elaine recognizes Kymancy — Detect Memory, a spell that
taps the history of a place and shows it. She understands the Wyrshade didn't
just attack the researchers; it showed them something and made them look. She
knows the Throng hive mind from Melody's Book 1 intelligence and the
Scaleblades — and can't say what she knows.

**The Danger:** *Dangerous to her enemies and to herself* — the truth Tella
didn't say, whispered by Phoebe. **[TBD]** The mechanism of self-harm (what the
mark costs Elaine — sanity, self, boundaries between her feelings and others')
is not yet specified in the roadmap.
