## Candidate Shapes **[PROPOSED]**

1. **The Departure Decision.** The pressure that forces the Promise to accept
   the poisoned contract (Quest crisis) while Brunor's silence finally
   detonates (Constellation crisis) and Elaine's withheld knowledge is exposed
   or costs a life (Fire crisis). One strand is sacrificed: the trio departs
   for Vel'Era with a bond broken or a truth burned.
2. **The Order Strikes First.** Brunor's hunt (Ch 6) provokes the aware
   infrastructure into retaliation inside Glintgate — forcing a choice between
   protecting their standing with the Council, their leverage against Damien,
   or each other.

## Requirements (from the Crucible Structure)

- The choice must be impossible: both options cost something real
- The loss must be permanent and traceable through the rest of the book
- All three strands must be present in the scene
